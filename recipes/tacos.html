Tacos

Description

Taco's are gods gift to man. Here is a recipe for them

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground turkey
  • Taco seasoning
  • Garnishes
  • Blue Cheese
  • Feta

Steps

  1. Brown Meat
  2. Once meat is browned, add taco seasoning and fill the pan with water till it covers the meat. Let the water boil off
  3. Once water is boiled off, add meet, cheese and garnishes to a tortilla as you wish
  4. Consume the taco
