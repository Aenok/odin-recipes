Mac & Cheese

Description

Its pasta and cheese - what else is there to say?

Ingredients

  • 1 package of elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • Other stuff

Steps

  1. Boil pasta. Stir in macaroni. Cook it and drain it
  2. Add butter to pasta. Season with salt and pepper white boy. Sprinkle half the cheddar in
  3. Finish cooking the pasta
